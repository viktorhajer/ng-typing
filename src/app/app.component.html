Ng-Typing Demo

Default (normal speed, less typo):
<typing [message]="..."></typing>
Quick with typo:
<typing [message]="..." [referenceSpeed]="50" [typo]="true" [typoProbability]="20"></typing>
Slow without typo:
<typing [message]="..." [referenceSpeed]="200" [typo]="false"></typing>
Quick with too many typing errors:
<typing [message]="..." [referenceSpeed]="15" [typo]="true" [typoProbability]="70"></typing>
